Data Deletion

Avii is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not create user accounts or store your personal data (including face tracking data) on our servers.

No Server Data: Since we do not have a user database or store your facial data in the cloud, there is no remote account or data to delete request from us.

How to Delete Local Data

All application data (such as settings or cached models) is stored locally on your device. To delete this data, simply:

  1. Uninstall the Avii application from your device.
  2. This will permanently remove all local data associated with the app.

数据删除

Avii 极其重视您的隐私。我们 不会 创建用户账号，也不会在我们的服务器上存储您的个人数据（包括面部追踪数据）。

无服务器数据： 由于我们没有建立用户数据库，也不在云端存储您的面部数据，因此无需向我们提交远程数据删除请求。

如何删除本地数据

所有的应用数据（例如设置或缓存的模型）均存储在您的设备本地。如需删除这些数据，您只需：

  1. 从您的设备上卸载 Avii 应用程序。
  2. 只需这一步操作，所有与应用相关的本地数据即被永久移除。
