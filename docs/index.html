Features

Live2D Rendering

High-performance Live2D model rendering based on PIXI.js, bringing smooth and responsive animations to your device.

Face Tracking

Real-time face tracking powered by Native ARKit (iOS) and MediaPipe (Web/Android), ensuring your avatar mirrors your expressions perfectly.

Privacy First

Face tracking data is processed locally on your device. We do not store or transmit your camera data or facial features.

Cross Platform

Built with .NET MAUI and Ionic, Avii delivers a consistent experience across iOS and Android devices.
